About us

Student-led aerospace, built at UoN.

We bring together students from engineering, software, electrical, mechanical, aerospace, and technology backgrounds to work on real rocket systems from concept to launch.

What we do

NU Rocketry designs, builds, tests, and launches high-power rockets. The team works across airframes, propulsion research, avionics, telemetry, recovery systems, payloads, testing, manufacturing, and competition preparation.

Why it matters

The team gives students hands-on experience that is difficult to get in a classroom: real design constraints, real engineering failures, real testing, and real launches.
