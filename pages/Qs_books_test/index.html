About Q’s Books

Opened in 1996 by Brian and Aileen Querruel, Q’s Books has been part of Newcastle’s book‑loving community for decades. Today, you’ll find us on Beaumont Street in Hamilton, keeping pre‑loved books in circulation and helping readers discover their next favourite.

Sell & Trade‑in

Downsizing a collection or bringing in a few paperbacks? We buy selected titles based on condition and demand. Bring them in during opening hours for a quick look — larger collections by appointment.

  • Clean, un‑damaged books are most likely to be accepted.
  • We cannot accept mouldy, water‑damaged, or smoke‑affected books.
  • Please call ahead for bulk or estate collections.
